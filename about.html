About our brand

Pringles is an American brand of stackable potato-based crisps. Originally sold by Procter & Gamble (P&G) in 1968 and marketed as "Pringle's Newfangled Potato Chips", the brand was sold in 2012 to the current owners, Kellogg's.

Nowadays pringles can be bought in the most locals stores and is also one of the most popular chips/crisps in the world ever.

Pringles comes in many flavours such as original sweet paprika and ketchup. If you wanna know more head over to the products section.
